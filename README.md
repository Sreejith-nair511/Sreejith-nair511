## About

Game developer and AI engineer focused on two things: crafting interactive worlds that feel alive, and building systems that make them smarter. Founder of **JuGaad.exe** (trading as ZreeGames), an indie studio with 25+ published titles. Active collaborator on ISRO space tech projects. National champion at E-MINDS 2024 — 500+ competing teams, 28-hour marathon, first place.

Currently deep in a Steam RPG with AI-driven NPCs, a real-time satellite tracking system for ISRO, and research into adaptive AI companions for games.
